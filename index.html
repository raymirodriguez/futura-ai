Featured Product Pulse

AI Voice Receptionist

The first flagship product from Futura AI Solutions. Pulse answers missed calls, books appointments through Google Calendar, and sends instant WhatsApp summaries to your team. Built for businesses that cannot afford to lose leads when no one picks up the phone.

Your team handles the customers in front of them. Pulse handles the calls that would otherwise go unanswered.

01

Your phone rings

Staff have 4 rings to answer. If no one picks up, Pulse activates automatically — no call forwarding setup required.

02

Pulse answers

A natural, professional AI voice handles the conversation in Spanish, English, or both — answering questions and qualifying the caller.

03

Appointment booked

Pulse checks your Google Calendar in real time and confirms the booking directly. No manual entry. No double bookings.

04

WhatsApp summary sent

Within 60 seconds of the call ending, Pulse sends a full summary to your team on WhatsApp. The caller gets a confirmation too.
