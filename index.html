The Problem

The Silent Revenue Leak

62% of calls to small businesses go unanswered. 85% of those callers never call back. The revenue loss is real, measurable, and invisible — until now.

Missed calls = lost customers

Every unanswered call is a potential client moving on to your competitor. Most SMBs lose 3–10 bookings per week without ever knowing it.

After-hours is the worst gap

40% of service calls arrive outside business hours. Without an AI receptionist, every ring goes to voicemail — or rings out entirely.

A human receptionist isn't the answer

A receptionist costs $400–800 USD/month, works one shift, and can't cover evenings or weekends. There's a better way.

The math is simple

If your average client is worth $300 and you recover just 2 missed bookings per month, the AI Receptionist pays for itself — everything else is pure upside.
